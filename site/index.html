How heat is scored

A deliberately narrow, objective signal — no topic keywords and no editorial priority. It measures only whether the exact document is being surfaced publicly.

  • 6× exact-URL mentions
  • 3× canonical-URL variants
  • 2× filename mentions (capped at 5)
  • 2× social exact mentions

Windows

Three rolling windows are recomputed every run — today, 7d, and 30d — across the whole inventory. A document is never dropped just because it scrolled off a source's recent page.
